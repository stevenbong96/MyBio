About Me

Hello!
My name is Steven Bong and I'm from Jakarta, Indonesia. For those of you who don't know, Indonesia is located in South East Asia and has tropical season all year long. I spent my whole time in Jakarta since I was born. In 2014, I came to USA to continue my study at Edmonds Community College in Edmonds, WA. Then, after I got my AA degree, I transferred down to Oregon State University in 2016 to purse my Bachelor's Degree. However, a year later, I decided to transfer to Texas A&M University for my final destination before I graduated.

I chose Texas because I have never been there before and would like to explore more in MidWest / SouthWest area, but things did not go pretty smooth after I graduated since I wasn't able to get a job in Texas. That's why I decided to move back to Seattle due to couple reasons and one of them is due to the extreme weather in Texas (FYI: Temperature in Texas during summer could reach up to 110 F!!!!). Well, that's a little bit of myself and my past journey as a student in USA! In the portfolio section, you will find couple of photos about me
